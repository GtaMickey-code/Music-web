About GTA Mickey

The story behind the name, the sound, and the movement.

From the Underground to the Spotlight

GTA Mickey is a rising force in the music scene, blending raw street energy with melodic storytelling that hits different. Known for tracks like Thugs Cry Too, Inner Energy, and Get The Smoke, his sound captures the grit and emotion of real-life experiences — unfiltered, unapologetic, and undeniably authentic.

Collaborating with artists like Oga BG and 18illest, GTA Mickey has carved out a space where vulnerability meets power. His music doesn't just play — it resonates. Every verse is a chapter, every hook a feeling, every track a testament to the hustle.

With a growing catalog on Spotify, Apple Music, SoundCloud, and Audiomack, GTA Mickey is building more than a fanbase — he's building a movement. The name isn't just a brand. It's a statement. This is GTA Mickey. Welcome to the ride.
